# Code of Conduct

Threadway is committed to providing a welcoming and harassment-free experience
for everyone who interacts with our projects, our community spaces, and our
team — regardless of background, identity, or experience level.

We follow the **[Contributor Covenant, version 2.1](https://www.contributor-covenant.org/version/2/1/code_of_conduct/)**
as our standard for community conduct. The full text describes the behaviour
we expect and the behaviour we don't tolerate; please read it.

## In short

- Be respectful. Assume good faith. Disagree with ideas, not people.
- Don't harass, threaten, or demean anyone, in public or in private.
- If something feels off, raise it — silence helps no one.

## Reporting

If you experience or witness behaviour that violates this code — in any
Threadway-operated space (GitHub, email, video calls, in-person events) —
please report it to **[adam@threadway.ai](mailto:adam@threadway.ai)**.

Reports are read by Threadway's leadership and handled with discretion. You
will not face retaliation for reporting in good faith. We will:

1. Acknowledge your report within 3 business days.
2. Investigate confidentially and follow up with you on the outcome.
3. Take whatever action is needed to keep the community safe, up to and
   including removing people from Threadway projects and spaces.

## Scope

This code applies to all Threadway community spaces and to behaviour outside
those spaces when it affects them or their members.
